✨ Spirited Away

★★★★★ 4.9 / 5

📅 Release: 2001

🎬 Director: Hayao Miyazaki

🎭 Genre: Fantasy • Adventure

⏱ Runtime: 125 minutes

📖 Synopsis

Chihiro is a ten-year-old girl who enters a mysterious world of spirits after her parents are transformed into pigs. She must find courage, work in a magical bathhouse and discover her inner strength to save her family and return to the human world.

✨ Fun Facts

  • It won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature in 2003.
  • It is one of the highest-grossing films in Japanese history.
  • No-Face is one of Studio Ghibli's most iconic characters.
  • Many critics consider it one of the greatest animated films ever made.
