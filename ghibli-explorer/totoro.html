🌳 My Neighbor Totoro

★★★★★ 4.8 / 5

📅 Release: 1988

🎬 Director: Hayao Miyazaki

🎭 Genre: Fantasy • Family

⏱ Runtime: 86 minutes

📖 Synopsis

Sisters Satsuki and Mei move to the countryside with their father while their mother recovers in hospital. During their adventures they meet Totoro, a gentle forest spirit who introduces them to a magical world filled with wonder, friendship and imagination.

🌿 Fun Facts

  • Totoro is the official mascot of Studio Ghibli.
  • The movie premiered in 1988.
  • It celebrates nature, family and childhood.
  • It is one of Hayao Miyazaki's most famous films.
