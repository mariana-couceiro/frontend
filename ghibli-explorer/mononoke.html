🐺 Princess Mononoke

★★★★★ 4.9 / 5

📅 Release: 1997

🎬 Director: Hayao Miyazaki

🎭 Genre: Fantasy • Adventure • Drama

⏱ Runtime: 133 minutes

📖 Synopsis

Prince Ashitaka is cursed after defending his village from a demon. Seeking a cure, he travels west and becomes caught in a conflict between the people of Iron Town and the spirits of the forest. There he meets San, the fearless Princess Mononoke, who fights to protect nature from human destruction.

🐺 Fun Facts

  • Princess Mononoke was Studio Ghibli's highest-grossing film in Japan until Spirited Away was released.
  • The story explores the balance between nature and human progress.
  • San was raised by wolves and is one of Studio Ghibli's strongest heroines.
  • The Forest Spirit is one of the most iconic magical creatures in Ghibli films.
