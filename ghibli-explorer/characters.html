Meet the Characters

Totoro

A gentle forest spirit and one of Studio Ghibli's most iconic characters.

Chihiro

A brave and kind girl who enters the mysterious spirit world in Spirited Away.

Haku

A mysterious river spirit who helps Chihiro on her unforgettable adventure.

Howl

A powerful wizard who lives in a magical moving castle and protects those he loves.

Kiki

A cheerful young witch who starts her own delivery service while learning to be independent.

No-Face

A mysterious spirit from Spirited Away whose behaviour changes depending on those around him.
