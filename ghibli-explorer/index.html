About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is one of the world's most famous animation studios, founded in Japan in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata and Toshio Suzuki.

Ghibli films are loved for their beautiful animation, unforgettable characters and inspiring stories that celebrate nature, friendship, courage and imagination.
